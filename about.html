The Remarkable History of Nyabigena

Is there a school in Kisii County as deeply rooted in both excellence and history as Nyabigena? Established in the early 1980s, Nyabigena Boys High School began as a small institution, brimming with hope and ambition. Over the years, it grew not just in size but in reputation, earning the respect of educators and students alike for its unwavering commitment to academic and moral excellence.
